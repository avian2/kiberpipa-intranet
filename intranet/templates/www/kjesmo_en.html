Kiberpipa is located in the basement of Cafe Metropol on Kersnikova street 6 in Ljubljana, which is in the very center of the city, and is easily accessible (it is in close vicinity to Ljubljana's main railway station, main bus station and public parking garages). A lot of youngsters and students hang out in the area as it is just next door to SOU(Slovenian student organization), Klub K4 , the Kapelica G allery and the City Library.
